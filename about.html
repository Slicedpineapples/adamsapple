I graduated from the Budapest University of Technology, Faculty of Architecture in 1989. Since then, I have continuously been working as a professional architect. Over the past 25 years I have designed over 200 buildings across Hungary and in the neighbouring countries. I use the experience I have gained during this period for the benefit of my clients every day. My projects cover a diverse set of areas: conventional detached and semi-detached, passive and terraced houses, apartment buildings, public projects and industrial complexes. Furthermore, I often get involved in the interior design part of the planning process too. During an assignment, I oversee the complete process from conceptual planning, through obtaining all necessary permits, to the implementation of the plans. Thus I see my role as a partner for my clients, someone they can always rely on.This is also why I believe that:The architect is not a sculptor, or a painter, who create for their own fame.
She is only a servant, who influences others' life by creating their living space in the way it's best for them.

Andrea Toth
